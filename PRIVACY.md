# Privacy Policy for Lunar

**Last Updated: February 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Lunar ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and profile details.
- **User Content**: Post listings, messages, and ratings in the Bazaar and Spaces features.
- **Location Information**: We use your location to find nearby halal restaurants.
- **Device Information**: Information about your mobile device and app usage.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain our service.
- To notify you about changes to our service.
- To allow you to participate in interactive features.
- To provide customer support.

## 3. Data Storage
We use Supabase for data storage and authentication. Your data is stored securely in accordance with their privacy practices.

## 4. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account within the app at any time.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
